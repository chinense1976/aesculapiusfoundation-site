Support Our Mission

The Aesculapius Foundation is reactivating and expanding its mission to advance healthcare education, veterinary diagnostic literacy, One Health awareness, professional development, and community empowerment.

Support for the Foundation helps make high-quality educational resources, independent validation and product evaluation initiatives, and access programs more available to healthcare and veterinary professionals, students, caregivers, practices, and communities.

Educational Resources

Support may help fund accessible health and veterinary diagnostic education materials, resource guides, learning tools, and community-facing educational content.

Validation & Product Evaluation

Support may help make independent, non-commercial validation and product evaluation data available to veterinary practices and educators without charge.

Professional Development

Support may help make lectures, workshops, seminars, and learning opportunities available to healthcare and veterinary professionals.

Access & Equity

Support may help expand diagnostic education and instrument-access initiatives for underserved settings.

Donations
