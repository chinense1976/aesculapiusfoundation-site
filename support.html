Support Our Mission

The Aesculapius Foundation is reactivating its mission to advance healthcare education, whole-person health literacy, professional development, and community empowerment.

Support for the Foundation helps make high-quality educational resources and initiatives more accessible to healthcare professionals, students, caregivers, and communities.

Educational Resources

Support may help fund accessible health education materials, resource guides, learning tools, and community-facing educational content.

Professional Development

Support may help make lectures, workshops, seminars, and learning opportunities available to healthcare professionals and students.

Community Health Literacy

Support may help expand educational initiatives that help individuals and communities engage more confidently with healthcare systems.

Program Development

Support may help fund planning, needs assessment, faculty coordination, educational design, program management, and evaluation.

Donations

The Aesculapius Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Contributions may be tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.
