Access & Equity Equipment Grant Program

Helping qualified organizations expand access to diagnostic tools, veterinary supplies, and educational resources.

Program Overview

The Aesculapius Foundation's Access & Equity Equipment Grant Program is designed to support qualified organizations that serve underserved animals, communities, or veterinary care settings. Through this program, eligible applicants may request donated or grant-supported diagnostic equipment, supplies, testing materials, or educational resources that align with the Foundation's mission.

Requests are reviewed case by case. Submission of a request does not guarantee approval. Awards depend on available funding, donated equipment, partner support, program priorities, and eligibility review.

Who May Apply

The program is intended for organizations whose work expands access to veterinary care, diagnostic capacity, or community health education.

Animal Shelters & Rescues

Organizations providing direct care, triage, or placement services for animals in need.

HVLC Clinics

High-volume low-cost veterinary clinics serving communities with limited access to care.

Rural or Mobile Practices

Veterinary teams serving rural, remote, mobile, or resource-limited settings.

Educational Institutions

Veterinary schools, teaching programs, or training organizations with mission-aligned needs.

Community & One Health Partners

Organizations addressing animal health, community health, or One Health access gaps.

What Organizations May Request

  • Veterinary diagnostic instruments or point-of-care testing tools
  • Testing supplies, consumables, or sample-handling materials
  • Veterinary medical supplies aligned with access-to-care goals
  • Educational materials, training resources, or implementation support
  • Other mission-aligned equipment or supply needs

Review Criteria

Requests may be evaluated based on community need, population served, organizational capacity, intended use, ability to maintain and responsibly use equipment, expected impact, and alignment with the Foundation's charitable and educational purposes.

Important: Grant support is not an entitlement and should not be represented as guaranteed funding, free equipment, or automatic approval.

Submit a Grant Request

Qualified organizations may submit a formal grant request through the Foundation's Access & Equity Equipment Grant Request form. The form collects organization details, nonprofit or service status, population served, requested equipment or supplies, intended use, need statement, expected impact, and maintenance capacity.

Requests are submitted securely through Google Forms and logged in the Foundation's response system.

Submit a Grant RequestSupport This Program

The grant request form opens in a new tab on Google Forms. If you have an urgent inquiry, contact contact@aesculapiusfoundation.org.

Program Independence and Stewardship

The Foundation is committed to responsible stewardship of donated funds, equipment, supplies, and partner-supported resources. Grant decisions are made in alignment with the Foundation's mission and charitable purposes. When equipment or supplies are provided through partner support, the Foundation will seek to preserve appropriate independence, transparency, and educational integrity.

The Aesculapius Foundation

The Aesculapius Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing healthcare education, veterinary diagnostic literacy, One Health awareness, professional development, and community empowerment.
