Educational Purpose

The Aesculapius Foundation provides educational resources and does not provide medical or veterinary diagnosis, treatment, or individualized clinical advice.

The Aesculapius Foundation

The Aesculapius Foundation advances One Health education, veterinary diagnostic literacy, and infectious disease preparedness so professionals and communities can detect disease earlier, make better care decisions, reduce avoidable costs, and protect human, animal, and environmental health.
